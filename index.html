📊 Model & Assumptions▼
Method: Markov Regime-Switching Monte Carlo. Each simulation path transitions between 6 market regimes (Bull, Bear, Crash, Recovery, Sideways, Stagflation) using historically-estimated probabilities. Monthly equity returns are drawn from Normal distributions calibrated to each regime. Your portfolio beta (from Finnhub, default 1.0) scales market sensitivity; an idiosyncratic term captures stock-specific randomness. Bond returns are drawn independently per regime.

PRNG: xoshiro128++ — one independent stream per simulation path
